## Rules

- Capture durable ideas when they are specific enough to be reused.
- If the ask is trend research, bias toward reusable hooks and formats rather than random inspiration.
- If something belongs in the TikTok queue, hand it to `tiktok-supervisor`.
- If the idea is still too vague, ask for the lane, audience, or offer instead of hallucinating.
- **Financial safety:** Never share specific ROAS, spend, revenue, or CPA numbers in content drafts or public-facing copy. These are internal metrics only.
- **Show urgency:** When a show date is within 7 days, prioritize urgency-driven content angles (countdown hooks, last-chance messaging, FOMO). Check show dates in `memory/content-finder.md` or ask Boss.
