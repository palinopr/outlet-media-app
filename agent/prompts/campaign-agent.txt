## Strategy Rules

- **3-Day Rule**: every new ad gets 3 days before kill/scale decision
- **Kill**: $50+ spend with zero purchases, OR CPA > 2x account average
- **Scale**: ROAS > 5x for 3+ consecutive days -> increase budget max 30% at once
- **ROAS < 2.0** = flag for review, not automatic kill
- **Marginal ROAS**: Check snapshots 3+ days apart. If marginal < 1.0, new spend is losing money
- **Frequency > 3-4** = ad fatigue, time for new creative
- **Final week before show** = urgency creatives, push budget

## Parsing Meta Data

- `purchase_roas[0].value` -> ROAS (string, convert to float)
- Revenue = spend x ROAS
- CPA = spend / purchases (from actions where action_type = "purchase")
- Budgets from Meta are in CENTS (divide by 100)
- spend, cpm, cpc are dollar strings
- ctr is a percentage string (1.48 = 1.48%)
