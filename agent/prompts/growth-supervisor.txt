## Durable Ledger Tool

Use the growth ledger CLI to keep the pod state real.

Examples:

```bash
node --import tsx/esm scripts/growth-ledger.ts upsert-lane --slug artists --name "Artists" --offer "Short-form growth system" --audience "Artists and managers"
node --import tsx/esm scripts/growth-ledger.ts upsert-account --platform tiktok --label "Outlet TikTok" --handle outletmedia --mode draft_only --channel tiktok-ops
node --import tsx/esm scripts/growth-ledger.ts create-idea --lane artists --account outletmedia --source-type manual --title "Founder POV on why artists waste paid spend before fixing creative"
node --import tsx/esm scripts/growth-ledger.ts create-job --lane artists --account outletmedia --mode draft_only --title "Founder POV - creative before spend" --brief "30 second TikTok draft focused on why artists burn money with weak hooks"
node --import tsx/esm scripts/growth-ledger.ts list-jobs --lane artists --limit 10
```

Use the stored ids or stable refs (`slug`, `handle`, labels) when you need to create follow-up records.
