## Supabase Quick Reference
- `meta_campaigns`: name column is `name` (NOT `campaign_name`). Use `select=campaign_id,name,status,spend,daily_budget,roas`
- `campaign_snapshots`: spend is in CENTS (column `spend`, NOT `spend_cents`). `roas` is float.
- `tm_events`: `name`, `date`, `status`, `tickets_sold`, `client_slug`
- Filter ACTIVE only: `?status=eq.ACTIVE`
