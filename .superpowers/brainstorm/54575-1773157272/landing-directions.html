Key differences

Direction A immediately shows the agents -- visitors see who's working for them. Best if "talk to your AI agents" is the hook that closes deals.
Direction B starts with why existing options fail, then reveals agents as the solution. Best if prospects are comparison-shopping and need to understand why this is different first.
